What is suicide and what are its causal factors?


          

Suicide is death caused by lethal self-harm. Some risk factors of suicide include: suicide attempts, misuse and abuse of alcohol and drugs, mental disorders (particularly depression and other mood disorders), access to lethal means, knowing someone who died by suicide (particularily a family member), social isolation, and chronic disease and disability.
